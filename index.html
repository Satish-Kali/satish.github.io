COMPETITIVE PROGRAMMER

Competitive programmers are all experienced programmers. Competitive programming, in contrast, covers some of the same skills taught in the computer science curriculum, but at a much deeper level..
Many technical recruiters including many in top-notch silicon valley companies like Google, Facebook, etc. use competitive programming as a yard-stick to measure the suitability of a candidate for the software engineering position. As a result people these days do competitive programming just to get a job. Not for fun.
